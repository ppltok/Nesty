Nesty Chrome Extension - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2024

Quick Summary: Nesty Extension only collects product information you choose to add to your registry. We do NOT track your browsing history, and we do NOT share your data with third parties.

Overview

Nesty Chrome Extension ("the Extension") is a companion tool for the Nesty baby registry web application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Product Information

When you use the Extension to add products to your registry, we collect:

  • Product name, price, and description
  • Product images and URLs
  • Store name and product URL
  • Categories and notes you add
  • Quantity and preferences (most wanted, private, etc.)

Authentication Data

  • We temporarily store your Nesty authentication session in Chrome's local storage
  • This session is used only to verify your identity when adding items to your registry
  • We do NOT store your password

How We Use Your Information

  • Product Data: Saved to your personal Nesty registry via Supabase database
  • Authentication: Used only to authenticate API requests to your registry
  • No Tracking: We do NOT track your browsing history or behavior
  • No Third Parties: We do NOT share your data with third parties

Data Storage

  • Authentication sessions are stored locally in Chrome storage
  • Product data is stored in Supabase (https://supabase.com)
  • All data is associated with your personal Nesty account
  • You can delete your data anytime by deleting items from your registry

Permissions Explanation

"Read and change all your data on all websites"

This permission is required to:

  • Extract product information from e-commerce sites when you click the extension icon
  • This ONLY activates when you explicitly click the Nesty icon on a product page
  • We do NOT access or read data from websites automatically

"Storage"

Required to cache your authentication session for faster performance.

"Tabs"

Required to detect if you have the Nesty web application open and are logged in.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we store about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your registry data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Contact

  • Website: https://ppltok.github.io/Nesty

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
